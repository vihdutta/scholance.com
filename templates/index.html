Earn community service hours online.

Get service hours and experience- all from your own home.


Whether you're completing service hours for school, or to gain experience, you've come to the right place.

Scholance is a platform that allows students of all backgrounds to complete community service hours online. With a focus on skill development, Scholance empowers students to take their communication, leadership, and computer science skills to infinity.

Plus, it'll always be free! Showcasing your stellar performance on Scholance could even help boost your college essays, project portfolios, and resumes- how cool is that?

Open Environment

We believe developers of all backgrounds deserve an opportunity to grow. Here at Scholance, you'll be accepted to projects indiscriminately.

Flexible Project Lengths

Community service can be long. Here at Scholance, we provide projects of all sizes.

Automatic Organization

It's hard to keep track of achievements. Here at Scholance, you'll receive an organized document of your work after every project.
