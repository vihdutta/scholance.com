About

What is Scholance?

Scholance, established in 2022, is committed to creating opportunities for students worldwide to actively participate in their communities. During the COVID-19 pandemic, the founders of Scholance recognized the difficulties students faced in fulfilling their community service requirements. Their search for virtual options to meet the mandatory minimum hours for graduation yielded no targeted online platforms for community service. Believing that community service should be easily accessible to students, even in the virtual world, they established Scholance to bridge this gap and provide students with an online platform that makes it easy and accessible to engage in community service. The founders' dedication and hard work paid off with numerous accolades, including the Congressional App Challenge 2022, a testament to the platform's impact on the lives of students and its ability to provide a platform for them to make a difference in the world.
